Sweet Apple Oat Crunch

Description

A sweet and savory yet nutritious breakfast meal that's super easy and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • Oats [40 g]
  • 1 whole apple
  • Yogurt
  • Cinnamon
  • Walnuts
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Grate half of the apple and pour the mushy substance into a plate.
  2. Add 2 large spoonfuls of yogurt to the apple mush and mix well.
  3. Sparkle some cinnamon on top of the yogurt/apple mixture.
  4. Add around 40 g of oats to your brown-looking mush. Mix and let it soak for about 5-10 minutes.
  5. In the meantime, chop the other half of the apple into tiny pieces.
  6. Add ‘some’ more oats, your diced apples, and chopped walnuts into a frying pan. Also add some more cinnamon.
  7. Spread the oat/apple mush flat on a plate and sprinkle the toasted ingredients from the pan above.
  8. Add another dollop of yogurt and drizzle with honey.
