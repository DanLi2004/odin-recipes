Fried Rice

Description

A sweet and savory yet nutritious breakfast meal that's super easy and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 25ml Light Soy Sauce (marinate in chicken - Brand: Ayam)
  • 300g Frozen Mixed Vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 Eggs & 100g Egg Whites (or just regular eggs)
  • 20g Spring Onion (white bits only)
  • 560g Cooked White Day Old Rice
  • 25ml Light Soy Sauce (add to fried rice)
  • 10ml Sesame Oil
  • 10g Spring Onion (green bits only)

Steps

  1. To a pan on high heat, add the 300g of frozen mixed vegetables.
  2. Add 2 eggs (mixed).
  3. Add the white bits of the spring onion.
  4. Add the white rice to the pan.
  5. Pour 25ml of soy sauce and take it off the heat.
  6. Add 10ml of sesame oil to the rice mixture.
  7. Add the green bits of the spring onion. MIX WELL.
